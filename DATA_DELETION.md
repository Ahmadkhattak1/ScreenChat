# ScreenChat Data Deletion

Last updated: March 14, 2026

You can request deletion of your ScreenChat account data by emailing `ahdfactz@gmail.com`.

Please include:

- The Google account email address you used with ScreenChat.
- The subject line `ScreenChat data deletion request`.

When a deletion request is verified, ScreenChat will delete the stored profile and saved chat history associated with that ScreenChat account. This does not delete your Google account itself.

If you only want to stop future use without deleting stored history, you can also sign out of ScreenChat from the extension profile view.
